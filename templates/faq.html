how does the queue system work?

When the timer expires, the first group in queue automatically moves to the court, and all other queue groups move up one position. Players previously on court need to re-queue once their time is up.

how do I join a game?

Click on any empty slot in an active court group or queue group to join. If there are no empty slots, you can create a new group by clicking the "Create New Group" button.

why can't I click on any empty slots?

You are either not logged in or you are already in an existing court/queue.

I'm currently on court, why can't I leave?

You cannot leave a court while the timer is running. This is to prevent players from queueing early while they are still playing.

I am currently on court X and want to move/swap to court Y to play with my friends, how can I do this?

If there is an empty spot, you can leave your current court/queue and rejoin on the court you are interested in. If you're looking to switch places with someone on another court, please ask a club officer for assistance. In general this would require all affected member(s) to agree to the switch.

I'm having some other kind of technical issues, what should I do?

First try refreshing (the page may lag on occasion). Otherwise, check that you are logged in, and that you are not already on a court. If there are any other questions please ask an admin.

this website sucks, I think I could do a better job, how can I contribute?

Feel free to either open a pull request or 'officially' join the team to help us maintain/improve the app! please reach out to a board member for details.
